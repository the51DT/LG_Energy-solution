I'm curious about the sustainability management policy

Lithium-ion battery cells manufactured by LG Energy Solution are sold only to industrial customers (e.g. battery pack manufacturers, system manufacturers, etc.).

LG Energy Solution does not sell lithium ion battery cells (i.e. 18650, single cell) to individual consumers to prevent accidents caused by handling lithium ion battery cells/pack and to use safe products.

Please do not purchase lithium-ion battery cells from LG Energy Solution that are distributed through e-commerce. For more information, please refer to the 'Lithium-ion Battery Safety Guide' posted on the website.
