Fair Trade

LG Energy Solution strictly manages a fair trade compliance system based on the CP (Compliance Program) to foster a culture of fair and transparent business practices. Through continuous compliance activities such as education, audits, and advisory programs, the company is enhancing and advancing its internal management system.

Fair Trade Compliance Program (CP)

The Compliance Program (CP) is an internal compliance system established and operated by companies to proactively prevent violations of fair trade laws. It includes the CEO’s commitment to compliance, the designation of a compliance officer responsible for program operations, monitoring systems for detecting potential violations, fair trade compliance training, and the publication of a compliance program handbook.

Eight Core Elements

  • 1

    Establishment and implementation of CP standards and procedures

  • 2

    CEO’s commitment and support for compliance

  • 3

    Appointment of a compliance officer responsible for CP operation

  • 4

    Development and utilization of a compliance handbook

  • 5

    Continuous and systematic compliance training

  • 6

    Establishment of an internal monitoring system

  • 7

    Disciplinary measures against employees who violate fair trade laws

  • 8

    Evaluation of effectiveness and implementation of improvement measures
