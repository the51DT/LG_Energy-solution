ESG Governance

LG Energy Solution established an ESG Committee in June 2021 as the highest governing body of ESG management to advance ESG agenda in a systematic manner.

  • We have also formed a dedicated ESG organization to strengthen internal expertise and capacity to address ESG issues and implement ESG strategic framework. It also allows us to meet the ESG-related demands from a wide range of stakeholders in a proactive manner. In addition, to manage ESG performance and accelerate the implementation in an effective manner, LG Energy Solution has formed an ESG Working Council consisting of members of relevant departments. The quarterly meetings of the Working Council serve as a platform to provide a comprehensive overview of ESG performance and exchange key issues and insights to implement the ESG strategic framework.

ESG Committee

  • Review and approve policies, strategies, and mid- to long-term objectives
  • Oversee the operation and implementation of policies and strategies
ESG Dpt.

ESG Policy Team

  • Establish strategic direction and implement key tasks
  • Manage global ESG initiatives
  • Respond to ESG assessments and conduct external communications
  • Operate the ESG Committee Secretariat and working-level councils

ESG Impact Team

  • Establish and implement carbon-negative strategie
  • Manage LCA and carbon footprint
  • Plan and operate CSR initiatives
  • Publish ESG reports
  • Embed ESG in employee practices
  • Manage supply chain ESG

ESG Working Council

  • Consists of members from departments implementing ESG vision and strategy
  • Monitor implementation of ESG strategic tasks and share insights

All employees

  • Complete on-site ESG training
  • Participate in and practice ESG management
