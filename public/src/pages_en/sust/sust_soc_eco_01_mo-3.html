Safety and Health

LG Energy Solution places occupational safety and health as a top management priority and pursues the continuous integration of safety and business operations by identifying and executing key initiatives that strengthen our capabilities and operational excellence in this area.

Safety and Health Strategy

LG Energy Solution has adopted a systematic occupational health and safety management system based on ISO 45001, and continues to improve it through third-party assessments and regular management reviews.

EH&S Management

LG Energy Solution publically announced its will for management based on safety, health and environment and established the EH&S management principles to suggest its overall directions. Based on these principles, LG Energy Solution established a set of standards for job implementation and rules/practices for work handling procedure to smoothly perform the works of business sites.

01

Policy

SE Policy shall be a top level document of management system that means corporation mission and direction officially announced by the CEO.

02

Standards

SE Standards shall regulate responsibilities, requirements and general procedures applicable to all sites.

03

Requirements

SE Requirements shall regulate specific methods or measures for detailed or general procedure set in Standards.

04

Procedures

SE Procedures shall be independent standards for individual sites including corporate HQ.Those Rules shall be practice document linked with SE regulations and practices.

EH&S Policy

Here at LG Energy Solution, we recognize that safety, health and environment(EH&S) is the fundamental element for securing differentiated competitiveness. For continuous improvement on EH&S performance, LG Energy Solution will be committed to implementing the following principles.

  1. 1

    We will comply with all applicable EH&S legal requirements and establish leading corporate EH&S rules and best practices.

  2. 2

    We will drive continuous innovation throughout the entire life cycle of operation to supply environmentally-friendly products and services.

  3. 3

    We will provide a safe and healthy work environment and ensure the principle-adhering corporate culture.

  4. 4

    We will support business partners and local communities to improve their EH&S practices as our social responsibility.

  5. 5

    We will communicate our EH&S policies and programs with stakehold

To ensure compliance with this policy, we will consider EH&S as the top priority in all ourbusiness activities.

Improvement of Safety Culture

LG Energy Solution is engaged in various activities to establish its own safety culture not only for facilities and equipment but also for workers.
  • Introduction of behavior-based safety (BBS) program: Change unstable behavior, which causes accidents, into safe behavior through observation and guidance between colleagues. Promote safe behavior through compliments.
  • Promote the leader's on-site management activities and each organization's own safe culture activities to prevent accidents.
  • Strengthen the on-site capability to prevent a recurrence of an accident by reflecting safety environment accidents in the evaluation of employees according to their gravity.

04 Work Environment Management

In order to ensure a safe and pleasant work environment, LG Energy Solution requests outside experts to assess harmful factors in the work environment every year The company reviews the work environment by establishing internal standards that are stronger than legal standards.
In addition, the company constantly makes efforts to ensure that all employees work in pleasant work environment by conducting audit and prevention activities for musculoskeletal disorders and conducting inspection of local exhaust systems.
