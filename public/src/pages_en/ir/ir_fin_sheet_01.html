Credit Rating Definition

Domestic Credit Rating Definition
Domestic Credit Rating Definition
Rating DEFINITION
AAA The highest likelihood of honoring financial obligations.
AA Very high likelihood of honoring financial obligations, but slightly lower than AAA.
A High likelihood of honoring financial obligations, but susceptible to changes in circumstances and in economic conditions.
BBB Moderate likelihood of honoring financial obligations, but more likely to be impaired by adverse economic conditions or changes in circumstances.
BB Some uncertainty over the likelihood of honoring financial obligations and speculative elements.
B Substantial uncertainty over the likelihood of honoring financial obligations and more speculative than BB.
CCC High risk of default on financial obligations and questionable likelihood of honoring financial obligations.
CC Very high risk of default on financial obligations and extremely low likelihood of honoring financial obligations
C Extremely high risk of default on financial obligations and lack of likelihood of honoring financial obligations.
D Dafault

'+' or '-' modifier can be attached to ratings through AA to B to differentiate ratings within broader rating categories.

ESG Bonds

ESG bond is issued for the purpose
of financing funds related to socially responsible investments such as Environment (E), Society (S), and Governance (G).

As a company conducting global business, responsible implementation of environmental and social roles is a prerequisite for sustainable growth.
As a global battery leader, LG Energy Solution is increasing its investment in businesses that create eco-friendly values through Green Financing.

LG Energy Solution’s Green framework has been certified by an independent external professional organization, and it has been rated as conforming to the principles of the International Capital Markets Association (ICMA) and the Lending Markets Association (LMA).

Green Bonds

Green bonds are bonds issued in the purpose of investing in businesses that create positive environmental values.

LG Energy Solution's Green Financing framework complies with the Green Bond Principles of the International Capital Market Association (ICMA) and the Green Loan Principles of the Loan Market Association (LMA).

The financial resources raised through Green Financing will be used for Low Carbon Transportation and Energy Efficiency projects.
