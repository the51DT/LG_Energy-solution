NCM Battery

NCM batteries are lithium-ion batteries that use lithium nickel-cobalt-manganese oxide (LiNiCoMnO₂) as the cathode material. By combining nickel (Ni), cobalt (Co), and manganese (Mn) in the cathode, they achieve an optimized balance of performance and stability.

Key Features

High Energy Density

  • As the nickel ratio increases, energy density rises
  • Delivering superior performance in EVs and electronic devices that demand high energy

Balanced Performance

  • Nickel (Ni): Enhances energy density and driving range
  • Cobalt (Co): Provides safety and prevents corrosion
  • Manganese (Mn): Contributes to thermal stability and extended lifespan

Flexible Design

  • Customizable with various nickel, cobalt, and manganese ratios (e.g., NCM111, NCM622, NCM811)
