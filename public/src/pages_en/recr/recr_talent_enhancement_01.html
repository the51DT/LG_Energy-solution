Ideal Candidates

With an aim to extending our capacities, LG Energy Solution inspires talents with growth, challenge, action, and collaboration who EMPOWER EVERY POSSIBILITY of the world with our technologies.

Growth

A Self-driven individual who seeks opportunities
of growth by acquiring and solving tasks.

Challenge

An individual who sets goals beyond the
expectation and strives for innovative methods
over adhering to the traditional ones.

Action

An individual that can execute decisions with
agility and fulfill tasks to completion.

Collaboration

An individual that keeps the common goals on
priority, and shows enthusiastic co-operation
while respecting the different background and
experience of colleagues.
