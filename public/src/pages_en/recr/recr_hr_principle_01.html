Fair and Transparent Performance Management

LG Energy Solution operates a fair and transparent performance management and pay system for all employees, including non-officers as well as non-sales department employees. Variable appraisal methods include continuous performance management, peer review, operation of Evaluation Talent Committee, which inform the provision of on-spot incentives (OSI) and regular personal incentives (PI). Further, it operates various capacity development programs, including job competency evaluation and consultation program for all employees and leadership survey and consultation program for managers.

Performance Evaluation

Our performance management is conducted in a fair and transparent manner through the following process: setting individual goals that are aligned with organizational goals, constant performance management, and year-end evaluation. To facilitate the effective management of and support to of members’ performances, we offer regular training and guidelines for leaders on performance management.

Peer Review

LG Energy Solution holds peer-to-peer feedback sessions once a year to improve the objectivity and fairness of evaluations and develop the competencies of our members by focusing on their strengths.

Job Competency Evaluation

LG Energy Solution conducts once a year job competency evaluation for all employees to enable them to strengthen their competency on their own and to manage human resources based on job competency. The results are reflected in the HR systems to inform selection process for promotion and training and development programs.

Leadership Surveys

Leadership surveys are conducted to assist leaders in identifying their strengths and areas for improvement, as well as to provide practical support for their leadership development and performance management. We collect feedback from leaders, seniors, colleagues, and other members and provide coaching to those who need it to help them develop their leadership skills.

Performance-based Compensation Programs

To ensure that employees feel valued and motivated, LG Energy Solution has implemented a strong performance-based incentive pay structure. This system is designed to align compensation with the actual performance and achievements of employees, thereby rewarding those who perform exceptionally. This incentive structure is comprehensive and inclusive, covering all levels (i.e. from entry-level, management to C-suite executives) and all functions (i.e. production, R&D, sales and others) of employees.
In addition to competitive base pay that accounts for individual performance, the employees are offered with various incentives including management performance incentives, personal incentives, and on- -spot incentives. This allows us to properly compensate our members for their contributions. LG Energy Solution complies with all compensation-related laws, including the minimum wage, and operates for all workforce without discrimination based on gender, nationality/ethnicity, religion, and social status.

Management performance incentives

Rewarded based on the company’s management performance (financial and competitive performance)

Personal Incentives(PI)

Rewarded differentially based on individual contribution

On-Spot Incentives(OSI)

Rewarded immediately to individuals at the time of their contribution

Talent Growth Framework

At LG Energy Solution, we believe that people are the source of differentiated competitiveness. Based on this belief, we aim to develop high-performing talent equipped with the capabilities of growth, challenge, execution, and collaboration. To support this goal, we operate structured learning and career development programs for employees worldwide, fostering continuous and sustainable innovation.
Through these initiatives, our people grow into top-tier professionals with core competencies, contributing to our vision of becoming a company that delivers truly differentiated and irreplaceable value. LG Energy Solution remains committed to providing robust support so that all employees across the globe can continue to grow and advance.

Development Programs

LG Energy Solution operates a global, tailored learning and development framework that supports employees’ career growth across three stages: Beginning, Growth, and Leadership. We provide a wide range of programs designed to build the competencies and experiences required at each stage, while also strengthening communication capabilities essential for a global work environment. Through this framework, employees are empowered to grow continuously alongside the company.

  • 1

    Beginning

    Onboarding for Successful Early Adaptation and Growth

    • Orientation Program

      New employees gain a clear understanding of LG Energy Solution’s talent values and organizational culture, while learning essential knowledge such as our value chain, policies, and work processes. This program helps them adapt smoothly and confidently to their roles.

    • IBT
      (Institute of Battery Technology)

      This course provides foundational knowledge of batteries—from basic principles and production processes to business structure and industry trends—enabling employees to build the core expertise required for roles in manufacturing and R&D.

    • On-the-Job Mentoring

      After joining their teams, new employees are paired with experienced colleagues for 1:1 mentoring. This program enhances practical understanding, strengthens workplace belonging, and supports a stable transition and sustained early growth.

  • 2

    Growth

    Developing Job Expertise and Advancing Career Growth

    Job Skill Enhancement (B-College)

    B-College provides structured learning designed to strengthen both technical and non-technical competencies, enabling employees to grow as experts in their respective fields. Training is offered across key functions, including:

    • Manufacturing / Engineering

    • R&D

    • Quality

    • Sales /Marketing

    • Procurement / SCM

    • Project Management

    • Strategy / Product Planning

    • Finance

    Career Level-Up

    This track expands each employee’s career vision by leveraging their experience and professional strengths. It supports both horizontal and vertical growth, helping individuals pursue their development aspirations through.

    • Growth Coaching (Leaders)

    • Career Coaching (Subject-Matter Experts)

    • Career Development Programs

  • 3

    Leadership

    Developing Future Leaders and Business Builders

    Business Builder Program

    This program supports employees with strong potential and ambition to develop the capabilities required to lead and drive new business opportunities.

    • Pre-Leadership

      Strengthens the ability to collaborate closely with leaders and contribute to organizational performance based on role expertise, while building a foundation for future leadership growth.

    • Leadership Onboarding

      Helps newly appointed leaders clearly understand their roles and responsibilities, enabling a successful role transition and the capability to lead their teams to high performance.

    • Leadership Build-Up

      Provides opportunities to assess and reflect on current leadership strengths and gaps, fostering continuous improvement and expanding the capabilities needed to grow into next-level leaders.
