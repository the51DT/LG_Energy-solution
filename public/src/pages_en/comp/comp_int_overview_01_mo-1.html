Through advanced products such as the “Long Cell” battery, which maximizes energy density and extends driving range, combined with our stable global production capabilities, we have established strategic partnerships with global top-tier automakers, leading the global EV market.

Beyond cells, modules, BMS (Battery Management System), and packs, our total battery solutions—including comprehensive technical support—are opening new markets for batteries in electric buses, trucks, ships, and urban air mobility (UAM), further advancing the innovation of future mobility.

With the world’s first freeform battery and high-performance standardized batteries, LG Energy Solution leads the small battery market by powering a wide range of products, including IT devices, home appliances, and LEVs (Light Electric Vehicles).

Leveraging advanced material technology and process innovations, our product capabilities meet the demands for high capacity, high power, and ultra-slim design. These strengths enable battery applications in future technologies such as drones, robots, and electric vehicles, contributing to the Battery of Things (BOT) era—where batteries power every aspect of daily life.

Through high-performance, high-quality batteries and mass production capabilities, we have established strong partnerships with customers across various ESS applications—including grid, commercial, and UPS—leading the ESS market while contributing to the wider deployment of renewable energy.

Building on our accumulated technology and expertise, we are also advancing reuse solutions that repurpose spent batteries into ESS batteries, playing a key role in establishing smart grids that enhance energy efficiency.

BaaS (Battery-as-a-Service) is a business that creates new value across the entire battery lifecycle. It encompasses the Battery Swapping Station (BSS) platform for electric two-wheelers, the B-Lifecare service that provides real-time monitoring for EV users, and the B.once service offering one-time battery evaluations. At the core lies BMTS, an advanced technology that goes beyond conventional BMS by integrating cloud and AI to enable intelligent safety diagnostics and precise prediction of battery degradation and lifespan.

EaaS integrates renewable energy resources into the grid. As a Virtual Power Plant (VPP) operator, LG Energy Solution participates in renewable energy bidding programs, contributing to the wider adoption of renewables and the stabilization of power systems.
