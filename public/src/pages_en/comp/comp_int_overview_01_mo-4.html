CI

The ‘Face of the Future’ which is the corporate symbol mark of LG Group represents the face of an energetic young person who constantly challenges and innovates for the world’s best.

This embodies the five concepts of world, future, youth, people, and technology. The letters L and G are inserted inside the circle signifying that people form the center of corporate management.
The logo represents the company’s pledge to maintain a close relationship with its customers around the world and in striving for customer satisfaction.

Logo

The Logo refers to the combination of the Symbol Mark and Logotype that serves to identify LG Energy Solution in all forms of communications.
The Symbol Mark cannot be used alone.
