G.Station

G. Station is a dedicated battery-sharing station for KooRoo.
The battery packs provided at the station are designed with enhanced performance and safety, delivering greater satisfaction to users.

Complying with Korean Industrial Standards
  • High reliability certified by national safety standards
  • Operating exclusive stations compatible with batteries compliant with Korean Industrial Standards
Improving User Convenience
  • Ergonomic design for improved user comfort
  • Multiple user verification methods offered (NFC, QR codes, etc.)
Reducing Battery Swap Time by 50%

Enhanced battery verification algorithms and station performance enable faster battery swapping

Extended Battery Slots

Battery slots can expand from the standard 10 to up to 30, depending on the station location

Advancing Safety and Performance
  • Improved temperature and humidity control
  • Enhanced fire and flood detection
  • Upgraded safety diagnostics and power interruption response
  • Continuous management service updates

G.Station Battery Packs

20% Higher Capacity

Comfortable and Secure Grip

Key Benefits

Optimized Station Operation

An AI-based system analyzes e-scooter rider data and battery swap frequency to optimize station placement, ensuring about a 1 km distance between stations. Stations are also strategically located in easily accessible spots, such as near convenience stores and supermarkets, to provide fast and convenient service.
