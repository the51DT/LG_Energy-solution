B-Lifecare

Overview

B-Lifecare, Korea’s first dedicated EV battery management solution, is a customized battery management app service that supports the entire lifecycle of a battery—from usage to diagnostics, management, and utilization—centered on the user.

B-Lifecare Website
Open in a new tab

B-Lifecare comprehensively analyzes EV users’ driving habits, charging methods, and driving conditions to provide key indicators such as real-time driving range, efficiency changes, and stress index. At the same time, it helps maintain battery performance while enhancing the overall EV user experience.
The service is currently expanding through collaborations with auto finance companies, vehicle maintenance providers, and used car distributors. Looking ahead, B-Lifecare aims to build an integrated lifecycle-based battery management ecosystem by connecting with insurers, charging infrastructure providers, and mobility platforms.

Service Offerings

Battery Certificate

Battery certificates can serve as quality guarantees in used car transactions, providing evaluation results such as maximum performance comparison and estimated market value.

Battery Care

Analysis of current capacity, safety status, and individual driving habits delivers insights into the battery’s future lifespan.

Driving Information

Provides recent driving distance, driving time, and efficiency data, along with estimated remaining driving range based on current battery charge.

Charging Habits

Analyzes personal charging patterns to recommend efficient charging times and guide better charging habits.

Nearby charging stations

Offers nearby charging station locations with details on fast and slow chargers.
