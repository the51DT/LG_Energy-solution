Overview

The power brokerage business is an energy trading service that connects renewable power plants with the electricity market through an ESS- and ICT-based Virtual Power Plant (VPP) platform. As the top broker in Jeju’srenewable energy bidding pilot project, LG Energy Solution has led various pioneering initiatives, including the industry’s first stand-alone ESS power plant connected to the distribution grid and demonstration projects that alleviate nighttime overloads using ESS. Through these efforts, the company is driving grid stability and advancing regulatory improvements.

EaaS

Energy-as-a-Service (EaaS) is a new paradigm that goes beyond simple energy production and consumption, delivering integrated services such as power generation, storage, trading, and optimization. Customers can entrust all aspects of energy management to the platform—free from operational complexity—while enjoying both efficiency and profitability. Leveraging AI-driven solutions and extensive experience in operating ESS and renewable energy systems worldwide, LG Energy Solution is expanding innovative energy services across global markets, including North America.

Service Offerings

VPP Operation

Integrating distributed energy resources into a single virtual power plant, providing functions such as generation forecasting, real-time power trading, and grid stabilization.

Power Brokerage & Bidding Agency

Delivering end-to-end support from developing customized bidding strategies to executing power market transactions on behalf of customers.

Renewable Energy Forecasting

Utilizing weather data, facility information, historical operation records, and AI models to deliver highly accurate generation forecasts.

Distribution Load Relief (NWAs)

By utilizing stand-alone ESS connected to the distribution grid, congestion can be bypassed and efficient power distribution enabled, thereby improving transmission and distribution efficiency while contributing to grid stability.
