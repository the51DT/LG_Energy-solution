Technology

Key Features

Battery Management Across the Entire Lifecycle

From manufacturing to usage, collection, and reuse, data is collected and analyzed throughout the entire lifecycle to extend battery lifespan and maximize residual value. Beyond simple monitoring, it enables the sustainable operation of battery assets.

Tailored Value Through Diverse Services

With a wide range of services such as B-Lifecare, KooRoo, leasing solutions, and one-time evaluation, LG Energy Solution provides optimized solutions tailored to customers’ usage environments and business models.

AI & Cloud-based Diagnostics and Forecasting

Advanced AI algorithms and cloud analytics platforms predict battery safety, performance degradation, and lifespan in advance, while providing optimal operating conditions. This not only prevents failures but also enables the development of efficient maintenance strategies.

Battery Asset Management & Operational Efficiency

By transforming battery condition histories and performance data into valuable assets, integration with various financial models such as leasing and insurance becomes possible. Battery replacement timing can also be scientifically managed to improve efficiency and asset value.

Core Technologies

BMTS(Battery Management Total Solution) is an innovative technology that goes beyond the role of a traditional BMS (Battery Management System), providing a comprehensive solution across the entire battery lifecycle. By integrating software, cloud technology, and artificial intelligence (AI), it delivers safer, simpler, and more efficient battery management for customers.

BMS

The Battery Management System (BMS) is a core control system that continuously monitors battery voltage, current, and temperature in real time, estimates the State of Charge (SoC) and State of Health (SoH), and maintains performance, lifespan, and safety through cell balancing.

Cloud BMS

As a global leader in better sector, LG Energy Solution has collected high-definition field data from over 20,000 EVs, securely stored on cloud servers. Continuous AI-ML-driven algorithm updates ensure enhanced analysis accuracy.

SDV BMS

SDV (Software Defined Vehicle) BMS is a next-generation battery management system optimized for software-defined vehicles (SDVs). By transferring BMS functions to high-performance computing (HPC) and leveraging AI-based diagnostics and control, it optimizes the functional architecture and reduces costs.

Safety Diagnostics Technology

Safety Diagnostics Technology is a software solution that detects and diagnoses various internal abnormalities of batteries in real time. To enhance the safety of EV batteries, it measures and analyzes battery information to detect potential issues that could lead to accidents such as fires in advance. By leveraging extensive field data, more than 130,000 cell disassembly analyses, and advanced AI technology, it achieves an accuracy rate exceeding 90%.

Degradation/Lifetime Prediction Technology

Degradation/Lifetime Prediction Technology diagnoses the current state of the battery, identifies and incorporates various factors affecting degradation, and based on this, accurately predicts future performance decline (degradation) and remaining useful life (RUL) with an error rate of just 1%.
