Lithium-ion Battery Safety Guide For Industrial Users

LG Energy Solution prioritizes customer safety and provides the following safety guidelines to prevent accidents caused by misuse of lithium-ion batteries.
Please strictly comply with this safety guide and the full guide attached. This guide applies to handling and using LG Energy Solution's lithium-ion battery units (cells/modules/packs) and systems equipped with these units (electric vehicles, energy storage systems, etc.), excluding ESS batteries.

This guide represents minimum requirements, and LG Energy Solution will not assume legal responsibility for any damage to products, fires, explosions, or resulting personal and property damage that occurs due to non-compliance with these guidelines. Please make sure to comply with the full safety guide attached.

The content of this safety guide may be periodically updated or revised as the scope of product environmental regulations expands.

1. Major Risks and Precautions

  • Lithium-ion Battery Unit contains intensive energy, and it can cause fire or explosion when misused or mishandled, leading to severe injury or property damage.
  • Lithium-ion battery cells/modules lack control functions itself, so they must be used in battery pack form.
  • Do not use lithium-ion battery units in applications not pre-approved by LG or in products not discussed with LG.

2. Precautions for Use and Handling

1. Precautions for Use
  • Use lithium-ion battery units only in pre-approved applications, and familiarize yourself with and comply with the latest version of the product specifications before use.
  • Do not use in environments with high static electricity, and wear protective equipment when handling.
  • Take care to prevent damage to the product during handling, verify the (+), (‐) tabs/terminals, and use them correctly.
2. Precautions for Charging
  • Strictly follow product specifications and charging manuals during charging, and use genuine chargers or globally standardized chargers officially certified by UL/IEC/EC/KC for charging.
  • If smoke, odor, swelling, discoloration, overheating, or other abnormal behavior occurs during charging, immediately stop charging and notify LG Energy Solution of the situation.
  • Do not charge for extended periods, and disconnect the charger after full charge.
3. Precautions for Storage
  • Store while strictly adhering to specified storage conditions (temperature/humidity, stacking count, etc.), and do not overload.
  • Insulate the tabs/terminals during storage, and do not store with charger connected or in fully charged/fully discharged state.
  • Keep the original packaging intact until use, and disconnect from the system if unused for extended periods.

3. Prohibitions

  • Do not disassemble or modify products without LG's permission.
  • Do not apply strong impacts, pressure, or heat to products, and do not expose products to moisture.
  • Do not force discharge products, and do not store products with metal objects.
  • Do not use damaged or dropped batteries.
  • Do not use battery products of different types, formats, sizes, specifications, etc. together.

4. Precautions for Lithium-Ion Battery Packs/Systems

1. Precautions for Battery Pack Assembly
  • Do not use batteries that have been subjected to impact or damage, or batteries showing external damage, leakage, odor, or rust for pack manufacturing.
  • Verify the (+), (‐) terminal condition to prevent short circuits, connect in the correct direction, and ensure terminals are not connected to conductors.
  • Do not mix lithium-ion battery units of different manufacturers, models, or specifications.
2. Battery Pack/System Requirements Battery pack manufacturers and system manufacturers must comply with the following rules :
  • Restrict individual consumers (or end-users) from using batteries with significantly reduced usage time or batteries that have reached EOL (End of Life).
  • Prevent individual consumers (or end-users) from recharging or using products when battery voltage falls below the minimum discharge voltage.
  • Equip battery packs with diagnostic functions by complying with contents specified in documents (check sheets, product specifications) discussed with LG.
    LG Energy Solution will not assume legal responsibility for all external quality issues resulting from exclusion/omission of functions agreed to be equipped.
